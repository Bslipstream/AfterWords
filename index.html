The Story Behind Afterthewords

I was sitting with a book I’d picked up from the library. I’m not a huge reader — I need a book to grab me instantly or I drift off. But this one… this one had me by page nine. It made me laugh, pause, feel something real.

I remember thinking, “I wish I’d bought this.” Not because I needed to own it, but because I wanted to show the author how much it meant to me. That’s when the idea hit me.

I didn’t buy the book… but surely I can show my appreciation somehow.

Afterthewords was born from that moment — a way for readers to leave gratitude, support authors, and help them continue creating the stories we love.
